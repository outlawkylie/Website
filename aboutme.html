Background

I am an undergraduate student at the Missouri Universty of Science and Technology. I am studying computer engineering with a minor in computer science. My interest in engineering began when I was young; math was always my favorite subject and I hoped to enter a field where I would always be learning something new. My mom and I went to a SWE camp the year before I began high school and I fell in love with engineering. In the future, I aspire to work with programming embedded systems, helping to create products that improve the everyday quality of life for people worldwide. I also hope to leverage my career in computer engineering to encourage young women to enter engineering.
